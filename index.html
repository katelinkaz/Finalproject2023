Budget Bites

Budget Bites was a group project that I completed with two classmates during a semester long course in User Interaction Design. We developed an app to solve users need for finding delicious meals with afforable ingredients that can be purchased at convient grocery stores. Through this project we conducted user research, created journey maps, personas, storyboards, and created screen images mockups to present the app's visuals. It was an exciting project that kept me on my toes and taught me how to execute UX/UI testing and design from beginning to end. Budget Bites Screen Images - Mockups Budget Bites User Research & More
